About


I am a recent graduate of SUNY Cortland, where I received a B.A. in communication studies with a concentration in public relations and advertising. Through my coursework I have become conditioned to understand the PR and advertising landscape hat companies are thriving in today.

As an active student amongst the Cortland community, I joined the Dragon Chronicle, a student-run newspaper, where I was a Staff Writer. My duties ensured that I was up-to-date in current news locally and nationally and that I kept our readers updated as well by writing weekly articles for publication.

While studying at Cortland, I was able to complete an internship at Pinckney Hugo Group, a full-service marketing communications firm. As a Public relations intern, I was able to gain hands on experience in a team-based environment. With no two days being the same, I was able to complete tasks ranging from writing press releases on new hires and building contact lists on Excel to searching for errors in a newly built website and coming up with new keywords on Google Ads.

Samples of my coursework and internship work can be found in the “Work Samples” section.

Recently, I have fully discovered my newfound love of technology. From Python to HTML/CSS, I am interested in the capabilities of writing code and the way it shapes the technology we use daily. I plan on expanding my knowledge on the technology landscape as a whole by enrolling in grad school and taking the next step to becoming a well-rounded young professional.
